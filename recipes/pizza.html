Pizza

Description

Homemade pizza is easy, fun, and customizable. With a crispy crust, tangy sauce, and melty cheese, pizza is a comfort food loved worldwide.

You can top it with your favorite veggies, meats, or keep it classic with just cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano
  • Your choice of toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Spread sauce over rolled-out dough.
  3. Sprinkle cheese and add toppings.
  4. Bake for 12–15 minutes until crust is golden.
  5. Slice and serve warm.
