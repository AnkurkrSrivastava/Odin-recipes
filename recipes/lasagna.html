Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a hearty Italian dish layered with pasta, meat sauce, and creamy cheese. Itâ€™s perfect for family dinners and special occasions.

The rich flavors and comforting texture make it a crowd-pleaser that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, onion, herbs

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles until tender.
  2. Cook ground beef with garlic, onion, and tomato sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake until golden and bubbling.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
