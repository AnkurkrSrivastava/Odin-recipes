Pancakes

Description

These fluffy pancakes are quick and easy to make. Perfect for breakfast or brunch, they taste amazing with maple syrup, butter, or fruit.

Theyâ€™re light, soft, and sure to brighten your morning.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk wet ingredients separately, then combine with dry.
  3. Heat a pan and lightly grease it.
  4. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  5. Serve warm with syrup or toppings of choice.
